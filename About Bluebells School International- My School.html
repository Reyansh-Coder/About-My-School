Bluebells School International

About Us

Bluebells School International was founded in 1957 by Hungarian educator Mari Guha and later managed by G. Soni. The school offers education from Nursery to Grade 12, and is affiliated with CBSE. Our mission is to provide holistic education and prepare global citizens who value peace, respect, and empathy.

Academics

Bluebells School provides a comprehensive curriculum designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. The curriculum includes Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams with facilities like Smart Classrooms, Science Labs, and a modern library. Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science), Commerce (Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies),Humanities (History, Geography, Political Science, Psychology)

Admissions

Admissions are conducted through a transparent process based on the neighborhood, alumni status, and available seats. Bluebells welcomes children aged 3-4 for Nursery. Senior grades depend on vacancy availability.

Extracurricular Activities

Bluebells School International encourages students to participate in a range of extracurricular activities, including - Debating , Theater , Music and Dance ,Art Exhibitions and Craft Workshops, Sports Meets, International Student Exchange Programs
